Our Foundation

More Than Karate

Shoukendou is a way of life. These four pillars guide everything we do on and off the mat.

Respect

For instructors, fellow students, opponents, and yourself. Respect is the foundation of all martial arts practice.

Honor

Act with integrity in and out of the dojo. What you practice here shapes who you become everywhere else.

Gratitude

Appreciate the opportunity to train, to grow, and to share this journey with your dojo family.

Pride

Take pride in your effort, your progress, and representing Tiger Den Dojo with excellence every day.

Meet Your Instructor

Sensei Brandon Chambers

Founder & Head Instructor — Tiger Den Dojo

Sensei Chambers is the founder of Tiger Den Dojo and creator of Shoukendou Karate — "The Way of the Honorable Fist." Drawing from years of dedicated training, he weaves techniques from Kempo, Shotokan, Taekwondo, and Kobudo into a cohesive, effective system.

His approach is personal and adaptive: he meets every student where they are, building a challenging yet supportive environment where beginners and advanced students alike can achieve their goals.

Students and parents consistently praise his patience, precision, and genuine investment in each person's success.
