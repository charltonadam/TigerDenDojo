Additional Fees

Beyond monthly tuition, here's what to expect as you progress through the program.

Item Details Cost
Startup Equipment Gi (uniform) + sparring gear set. Required for all except Tiny Tigers. $200–$300+
Tiny Tigers Uniform Dojo t-shirt only — no gi or gear required. Minimal
Belt Promotion — Yellow Stripe thru Purple Per testing fee when invited to promote. $45
Belt Promotion — Red thru Gray Per testing fee for advanced belts. $60
Black Belt Testing Comprehensive black belt evaluation. $500
Black Belt Club One-time membership fee at Red Belt and above. Required to continue toward black belt. $50 (one-time)
Tournament Entry Optional. Two in-school tournaments per year. External events vary. $40–$150+
Note: Tournaments are optional but encouraged for students ages 6 and up. Two in-school tournaments are held annually as a fun, low-pressure way to test your skills. You will only be invited to belt promotion when your instructor believes you are ready — there's no pressure to test on a fixed schedule.
Getting Started

What You'll Need

🐯 Tiny Tigers (3–5)

No special equipment required to start. Students wear a dojo t-shirt available through the dojo. Comfortable athletic shoes or bare feet are fine.

This is the most affordable program to get started with — just bring your child and let us handle the rest!

⚡ Cubs, Teens & Adults

For your two free trial classes, just wear comfortable athletic clothing. No gear needed.

Once you enroll, you'll need a gi (uniform) and a complete sparring gear set (helmet, chest protector, gloves, shin guards, mouthguard). Gear packages are available through the dojo for approximately $200–$300+.

🏅 Black Belt Candidates

Students pursuing their black belt will need quality running shoes and may wish to invest in upgraded gear, additional gis, and weapons (Kobudo) as they advance.

Sensei Chambers will guide you through equipment needs as you progress through the belt ranks.
