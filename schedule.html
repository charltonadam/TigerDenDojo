Getting Started

How to Schedule Your Trial

New students receive two free trial classes. Here's how to get started.

01

Choose Your Program

Select the age group that matches your child (or yourself) and the session option that fits your schedule.

02

Contact the Dojo

Call 801-808-4182 or email to confirm your spot. Drop-ins are not accepted.

03

Come Ready to Train

Wear comfortable athletic clothing. No gi or gear needed for your trial. Parents/guardians must be present for students under 18.

04

Decide & Enroll

After your two free classes, decide if Tiger Den Dojo is the right fit — no pressure, no commitment required.
